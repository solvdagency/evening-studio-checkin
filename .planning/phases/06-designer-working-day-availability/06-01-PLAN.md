<objective>
Change the single capacity choke point so available minutes for a day come from a designer's real rostered minutes, not the flat 450-minute constant — and thread that basis through `computeDesignerDay` and the rest-of-week rollup. This is the trust-critical arithmetic core of CAP-06: the domain contract that Plan 02 (Productive ingestion) produces data for and Plan 03 (rendering) reads results from.

Purpose: A designer on a non-standard week (e.g. Anisha off Wed & Fri) must never be flagged underbooked on a day she isn't rostered. The flat `TARGET_MINUTES − absence` baseline is the exact bug CAP-06 corrects.

Output: `availableMinutes` takes a rostered-minutes basis; `computeDesignerDay` and `computeStudioReport` thread per-designer per-day rostered minutes; the `"off"` status (available === 0) already handles a non-rostered day with NO new enum value (D-04); every change is pinned by exact-integer-minute `node:test` assertions.
</objective>
